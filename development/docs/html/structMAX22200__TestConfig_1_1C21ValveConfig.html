Detailed Description

Parker C21 valve Hit and Hold (compile-time CDR vs VDR)

Per C21 datasheet:

  • Hit: rated voltage; Hold: 50% of rated
  • Min hit time: 100 ms; PWM freq min: 1 kHz; Hold duty: 50%

Set to true to drive C21 with CDR (current regulation, low-side only); false for VDR (PWM duty: hit=100%, hold=50%).
